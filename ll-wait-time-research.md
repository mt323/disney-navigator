## LL Return Window Analysis

### How Return Windows Work
- You get "the next available window" — you don't pick a time
- Each window is 1 hour long
- After tapping in (or 2 hours after booking), you can book next
- Pro tip: Use "modify/refresh" trick to snag earlier cancelled slots

### Key Risks
- **Space Mountain** is the highest-demand LL at Disneyland — windows can push 2-3 hours out by mid-afternoon
- **Guardians** is highest-demand at DCA but first booking at entry usually gets a close window
- **Buzz Lightyear** barely needs LL (24 min standby) — often has immediate return
- **2-hour rule** is your safety net for pushed-out windows

### Disneyland Rides (Park 16)

**Space Mountain** (ride 284)
- Earliest slots: 8:00am available at 6:05am, sold out by 8:00
- Morning slots (8:00-9:00) sell out within 2 hours of appearing
- Afternoon slots (1:00-2:00pm) maintain longer availability
- Very high demand — hardest LL to get a close window

**Haunted Mansion** (ride 13958)
- Morning slots (8:00-9:55am): consistently available
- Sellout begins ~10:30-10:35am
- Peak unavailability: 10:35am-12:00pm
- Afternoon recovery: intermittent availability from 1:05pm
- Moderate demand

**Big Thunder Mountain** (ride 323)
- Slots sell out very quickly — 5-15 minute availability windows
- 8:00 slot gone by 8:15, 8:05 slot gone by 8:20
- Brief availability pops throughout the day
- High demand — slots fill faster than expected

**Tiana's Bayou Adventure** (ride 14168)
- Morning (6:00-10:20am): consistent availability
- Sellout begins ~10:30-10:35am
- Midday (10:35am-1:00pm): mostly unavailable
- Afternoon slots (1:00-2:15pm): scattered availability
- Moderate-high demand

**Matterhorn Bobsleds** (ride 279)
- Slots sell out within 5-15 minutes of appearing
- Morning slots gone fast (8:00 gone by 8:05)
- Second wave of availability around 2:00-2:15pm
- High demand

**Buzz Lightyear Astro Blasters** (ride 273)
- Morning (8:00-11:50am): ALL slots consistently available
- Sellout doesn't begin until ~12:10pm
- Afternoon: intermittent availability
- Low demand — easiest LL to get, often immediate return

### Implications for Our Plan (Monday March 16)

Your plan enters DCA at 11:00am. By that time on this Saturday:
- **Guardians** earliest window was ~12:25pm (morning slots long gone)
- **Incredicoaster** had a gap 10:45-12:05, but afternoon slots available
