## Space Mountain Risk & Mitigation

Space Mountain is LL #7, booked at ~3:48pm after tapping Big Thunder. Monday is SM's historically busiest day (49 min avg). By mid-afternoon, windows push 2-3 hours out.

**Mitigation: Modify/Refresh Trick.** After booking SM (even with a 5:30+ window), open the modify screen and repeatedly pull-to-refresh. Cancelled slots appear frequently. 5 min of patience = ~100% success rate at snagging an earlier window.

**If window pushes past 5:30pm:** The plan still works. Star Tours (4:18-4:48) fills the gap naturally. Ride SM when the window opens, then continue Matterhorn → Mr. Toad → Buzz → Galaxy's Edge. The 2-hour rule kicks in at ~5:48 as backup for booking Matterhorn.
